About Me

Sophomore at the University of Miami. Public Relations Major, Sports Administration Minor. Previously interned at Amerasport LLC doing design work and social media, current camp counselor at Camp Young Judaea. I like to travel and am fluent in Hebrew. I have been to Israel five times. That position helped me hone my leadership skills as well as allowed me to be a role model for younger high schoolers.
